# Gorilla Motors Website Proposal

## Prepared For
Gorilla Motors

## Project Summary
We propose a modern lead-generation website for Gorilla Motors that helps the business showcase available units, answer buyer questions faster, and convert more visitors into inquiries through Messenger, phone, and contact forms.

The current concept already has a strong direction. We recommend positioning this as a sales-focused dealership website with a clean mobile experience, clear calls to action, and simple tools that help customers browse units and request financing details.

## Project Goal
Build a professional website that:
- Presents Gorilla Motors as a trusted used vehicle dealer
- Makes it easy for buyers to browse available units
- Helps customers estimate monthly payments
- Captures leads for both buyers and sellers
- Works smoothly on mobile, where most traffic will likely come from

## Recommended Features

### 1. Brand-Focused Home Page
- Strong hero section with Gorilla Motors branding
- Trust-building highlights such as approval rate, units sold, and social proof
- Clear call-to-action buttons for Browse Inventory, Inquire Now, and Sell Your Vehicle

### 2. Vehicle Inventory Showcase
- Searchable and filterable vehicle listing section
- Categories by vehicle type and brand
- Featured units / hot deals section
- Price, year model, transmission, fuel type, and mileage display
- Initial upload of vehicle units provided by the client

### 3. Loan / Monthly Payment Calculator
- Interactive calculator for vehicle price, down payment, term, and interest
- Instant estimated monthly amortization
- Inquiry call-to-action after computation

### 4. Sell or Trade-In Lead Form
- Vehicle appraisal / submission form
- Fields for unit details, asking price, and contact information
- Designed to help Gorilla Motors capture seller leads directly from the site

### 5. Contact and Inquiry Conversion Tools
- Click-to-call buttons
- Messenger / Facebook inquiry buttons
- Contact section with location, phone numbers, and business details
- Inquiry form routing to email or preferred lead channel

### 6. Mobile-Responsive Design
- Optimized layout for phones, tablets, and desktop
- Fast-loading static website structure
- Simple user flow for browsing and contacting the dealership

### 7. Basic SEO and Launch Setup
- Search-friendly page titles and meta descriptions
- Google Maps / location linking
- Social sharing preview setup
- Deployment assistance for live launch

## Features We Can Add in the Recommended Version
If the client approves the higher end of the budget, these are the most valuable additions:
- Dedicated featured deals section
- Testimonials or buyer trust section
- FAQ section for financing and requirements
